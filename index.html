Tourism

The Dangs is known for its beautiful forests, hills, and waterfalls. It is one of the most peaceful and green regions in Gujarat.

Saputara Hill Station

Saputara is the only hill station in Gujarat, famous for its cool climate and scenic views.

Gira Waterfalls

Gira Waterfall is a popular tourist spot, especially during the monsoon season.

Culture

The Dangs has a rich tribal culture. Traditional dances, music, and clothing reflect their unique identity.

Local tribes celebrate their traditions through dance and festivals.

Festival

Festivals in the Dangs are full of joy and tradition, especially tribal fairs and celebrations.

The Dang Darbar festival is one of the most famous events in the region.

Food

The local food includes simple and traditional tribal dishes made with natural ingredients.

Food in the Dangs is healthy, organic, and deeply connected to nature.

Art

Tribal art and handmade crafts are an important part of the culture of the Dangs.

Handmade items and traditional art showcase local creativity.

Agriculture

Agriculture is the main occupation in the Dangs. Farmers grow crops like rice, maize, and vegetables.

Farming practices are mostly natural and eco-friendly.
